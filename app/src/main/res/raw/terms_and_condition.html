Volumetric Weight


The value of a shipment can be affected by the amount of space it occupies during distribution, rather than the actual weight which is known as volumetric (or dimensional) weight.

The volumetric or dimensional weight is calculated and compared with the actual weight of the shipment to ascertain which is greater; the higher weight is used to calculate the shipment cost.


Calculation

For Air Express & Air Cargo: FOR PACKING BOX

Length x Breadth x Height (in cms)
6000

For Surface:

Length x Breadth x Height (in cms)

4750

For International:

Length x Breadth x Height (in cms)
5000


If any dimension of the Consignments exceeds 110cms then 2 times the weight (Gross or Volumetric, whichever is more) is directly charged to the sender.


However, no provision for calculation of shipping cost to SENDER in terms of volumetric weight is available in the application. As such, it shall be TRAVELER’s responsibility to calculate the volumetric weight of the consignment and select appropriate weight bracket for actual calculation of pricing. In this regard, actual weight of the consignment or volumetric weight of the consignment, whichever is higher will be considered for actual pricing and SENDER shall be required to pay the cost as per weight bracket finalized by TRAVELER. The weight bracket finalized by TRAVELER at the time of start journey shall be the final and no modifications / disputes by any parties with regard to weight shall be entertained thereafter.
